How We Work

Eight out of ten people without access to food sources live in rural areas.From the start, that’s where we’ve focused our efforts(the rural areas). We work with carefully-chosen local partner organizations with one goal in mind which is ebnding food insecurity in Nigeria. But every great success starts small, so we believe we would grow and end food insecurity problems

Our Approach

We work with local partners to fund food, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) programs for rural communities around the world. But our work doesn’t stop when construction ends. We remain committed to monitoring, evaluation, and maintenance to ensure that these programs operate sustainably for years to come.

Learn More

Campaigns

Our local partners select areas where we go for campaigning, they choose locations based on a variety of factors like geography, assessment of need, community participation, and long-term sustainability.

Implementation

In addition to the construction process, local partners spend months establishing community buy-in, promoting safe hygiene practices, and engaging communities in enlightenment of food insecurity.

Post-Implementation

To ensure that food continues to flow for years to come, we empower local leaders, use strong operations and maintenance structures, give farmers more upgraded equipments that makes farming more easy.

We’re solution-agnostic

We also put in our goals to making and finding solutions in way to end food insecurity. We talk to government officials to provide upgraded tech equipments to make their works more eassy, fast and reliable. We also try to convince them in reducing the cost or increase rate of fuel.

Local Partners

Our carefully-chosen local partners have a community-centered approach, excellent reporting practices, and years of experience implementing effective, sustainable food security projects in their area.

Why does Jara Feeds Foundation work with local partners?

We believe that investing in local organizations is the best way to create long-term impact. With years of food insecured projects and hygiene experience and nuanced knowledge of the regions they serve, our local partners are true experts in their field.

What is Jara Feeds Foundation relationship with local partners?

Our food security Programs team communicates with our local partners every day to track progress and troubleshoot challenges. Without our global network of local partners and their dedicated staff, Jara Feeds Foundation work would not be possible.

How are local partners defined?

We work with a variety of locally-based organizations. Some are locally-founded nonprofits operating exclusively in one country. Others are branches of larger international NGOs with local chapters that serve individual countries or regions. In every case, local experts are designing, implementing, and monitoring the work.
