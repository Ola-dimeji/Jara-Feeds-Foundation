How We Work

We want you to know exactly how and where your donation is being used. So from the beginning,
we’ve kept a few promises.

We give 100%

100% of the money donated to Jara Feed Foundation goes directly to funding ending hunger projects. Private donors cover our operating costs, which means every penny of every dollar can go where it’s needed most.

We prove it

We don’t expect you to take our word for it. Our next-level commitment to tracking and transparency means we don’t just tell you you’re having an impact. We show you, with GPS coordinates and photos of the exact project(s) you’ve helped fund.

We deliver

Food insecurity affects millions of people around the world, leading to malnutrition, health problems, and a cycle of poverty that is hard to break. We believe that by tackling hunger head-on, we can make a significant impact on individuals' lives and contribute to the overall well-being of our communities to deliver a life worth-living.

We think local - and long-term

We don’t just drop in, provide foods for the needy and disappear. When we provide food for a community, we want the impact to be for good — which means we work closely with local partners, remaining invested in the integrity of projects for the long haul
