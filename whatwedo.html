About Us

Jara Feed Foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation committed to creating a world where no one goes hungry, and farmers are equipped to feed their communities sustainably. By empowering farmers, promoting sustainable practices, enhancing market access, and providing immediate food relief, we build resilient agricultural systems that ensure food security for all. Join us in our mission to fight against food insecurity and hunger, and help us make a lasting impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat food insecurity and hunger by empowering
farmers with the knowledge, tools, and resources they need to grow
more and better food, and by providing immediate food assistance
to those who are most vulnerable.

Ready to join us in creating a food secured society?

Our Vision

We envision a future where food insecurity and
hunger are eradicated. farmers are equipped with
the knowledge and resources to grow abundant,
high-quality crops using sustainable methods.
Every person, regardless of their location or
socioeconomic status, has access to
nutritious and sufficient food.
